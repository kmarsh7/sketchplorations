Trips to America

Exploring New York City

Activities

Activity 1: Natural History Museum, Food Halls
Activity 2: Metropolitain Museum of Art
Activity 3: Empire State Building and a Yankees Game
Activity 4: Statue of Liberty and Ellis Island
Activity 5: Freedom Tower and Wall Street
Activity 6: Boat Cruise Around NYC, Intrepid Museum
Activity 7: Central Park

New York City is the most heavily populated city in the US, and there are lots of museums and places to visit such as the Natural History and Metropolitan museums. Some of the most popular landmarks are the statue of liberty, the empire state building, and the freedom tower.

Adventure in Los Angeles

Activities

Activity 1: Climbing Hollywood Hills
Activity 2: Santa Monica Pier
Activity 3: Universal Studios
Activity 4: Malibu Waterfalls
Activity 5: Hollywood
Activity 6: Malibu Zoo
Activity 7: Watts Towers Art Center, and sports game

There are many things to do in the bustling city of Los Angeles. Climb the iconic Hollywood Hills and take pictures in front of the iconic Hollywood sign. Visit the famous Santa Monica pier and enjoy the sunshine while watching the trapeze artists train as the sun sets. In the following days, I would visit Universal Studios and explore all that the Cinematic capital of the world has to offer. Make sure to take a cruise down Rodeo drive and visit the celebrity stars on the Hollywood Walk of Fame as well. If time permits, take a hike to the Malibu waterfalls and visit the Giraffe in the local Malibu zoo. Wrapping up the trip means visiting the historic Watts towers - a national landmark - and making the trip downtown to the Staples center where LeBron James, Anthony Davis, and the Lakers play basketball.

Discovering Utah

Vacation 1: Skiing Utah's Mountains
Vacation 2: Touring Utah's National Parks

Utah is a fascinating state to visit in the US. It contains many great mountains piled with soft snow to ski on. It also is home to many species of wildlife and, Zion National Park, Bryce National Park, Arches National Park, and Canyonlands National Park.
